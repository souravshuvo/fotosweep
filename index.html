Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Foto Sweep handles information when you use the Foto Sweep mobile application. Foto Sweep is designed to help you review, organize, clean, and recover media and documents on your device.

Foto Sweep processes your photos, videos, documents, trash queue, and recovery scans on your device. We do not upload your personal photos, videos, or documents to our own servers.

Information Foto Sweep Accesses

Depending on the features you use and the permissions you grant, Foto Sweep may access:

  • Photos and videos, so the app can show your media library and help you select items to keep or move to trash.
  • Media metadata, such as file size, file type, date, and local media identifiers, so the app can sort, filter, and display storage information.
  • Documents and files in common folders, such as Downloads or Documents, when you use document browsing features.
  • Cached thumbnails and media traces on your device when you use Data Recovery or deep scan features.
  • App settings, such as onboarding status, haptic feedback preference, notification preference, and auto-group preference.

How Foto Sweep Uses Information

Foto Sweep uses on-device information to:

  • Display your photos, videos, and documents inside the app.
  • Sort and filter media by type, size, age, screenshots, blur, duplicates, or other cleanup views.
  • Track items you mark for deletion before you permanently delete them.
  • Recover or save available cached thumbnails or recoverable media copies when possible.
  • Store app preferences and improve the in-app cleanup workflow.
  • Show advertisements that keep Foto Sweep available without a premium purchase.

Local Storage and Deletion

Foto Sweep stores app data locally on your device, including your trash queue, recently deleted tracking information, session data, and settings. Recovered files may be saved to a local folder such as Pictures/CleanSwipe_Recovered or another device storage location shown by the app.

You can clear app data through your device settings, clear the trash queue inside the app, revoke permissions through Android settings, or uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data. Files already saved or recovered to device storage may need to be deleted manually from your device.

Advertisements and Third-Party Services

Foto Sweep uses Google AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK to display ads, including banner, app open, interstitial, and native ads. Google and its partners may collect and process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app information, ad interactions, crash or diagnostic data, and approximate location inferred from your IP address to deliver, measure, personalize, and protect ads from fraud and abuse.

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. You can also review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy and manage ad personalization at adssettings.google.com.

Permissions

Foto Sweep may request the following device permissions:

  • Photos and videos permission, to read and display media selected or available to the app.
  • Storage or file access permission, to browse documents, inspect file metadata, or save recovered files where supported by Android.
  • Notification permission, if reminders are enabled.
  • Vibration permission, to provide haptic feedback.
  • Internet access, to load advertisements and related ad services.

You can deny or revoke permissions in your device settings. Some features may not work without the permissions they require.

Data Sharing

Foto Sweep does not sell your personal photos, videos, or documents. Foto Sweep does not upload your media library to our own servers. Information may be shared with third-party service providers, such as Google AdMob, as described in the advertisements section above, or when required by law.

Children's Privacy

Foto Sweep is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information handled by the app. However, no app, device, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

  • Revoke photo, video, file, notification, or other permissions through your device settings.
  • Clear Foto Sweep app data through Android settings.
  • Delete recovered files manually from your device storage.
  • Control ad personalization through your Google account ad settings.
  • Uninstall Foto Sweep at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Foto Sweep after an update means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Foto Sweep's privacy practices, contact us using the developer support email listed on the Foto Sweep Google Play Store listing.
